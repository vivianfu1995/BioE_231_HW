            (other) R10619

COMMENT     Also acts on L-arabinose, D-xylose, D-galactose, maltose and lactose. This enzyme catalyses the first step in galactose metabolism by converting beta-D-galactose into alpha-D-galactose, which is the substrate for EC 2.7.1.6, galactokinase [5,6].
